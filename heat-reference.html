What is SHU?

Scoville Heat Units measure the concentration of capsaicin, the chemical compound that creates the burning sensation. The higher the SHU, the hotter the pepper.

How It's Measured

Originally tested by diluting pepper extract until heat was no longer detectable. Modern testing uses high-performance liquid chromatography for precision.
